Meet the Advisors

Founder

Julieta Reyes

Strategic Advisor | Diversity & Inclusion Lead | Global Operations

19 years at the highest levels of international institutional life in diplomatic operations, public and military diplomacy, nation branding, strategic communications, and organizational development. Fluent in four languages.

Her career took shape within the Kingdom of the Netherlands' diplomatic missions in the United States: first at the Consulate General in Miami, and later at the Embassy in Washington, D.C., the capital of the Free World. For years, she acted as a bridge between diverse organizations like the foreign missions of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Defense, giving her a great understanding of the complex relationships within the Kingdom of the Netherlands. She built expertise in strategic communication, inclusive protocol design, and logistics modernization, serving as a trusted Chief of Support to military leadership and heads of mission. She is specialized in strengthening the "human infrastructure" of organizations, facilitating knowledge sharing and workshops that turn challenges into collaborative opportunities.

Advisor

Ryan Barras

Director of IT Security / CISO | GRC & Strategic Operations

Healthcare, government, and corporate sectors. Builds security programs that protect data integrity and business continuity without compromising innovation.

Expertise & Specializations: Third-party risk management (TPRM) program maturation, Security Operations Center (SecOps) automation via ServiceNow, executive-level CISA-aligned tabletop exercises and crisis simulations, and enterprise security governance for Fortune 500 and public institutions.

Leadership Experience: Mt. Sinai Health System (CISO-level roles), US Department of Labor (consulting), Princeton University, Blue Cross Blue Shield. Ryan's defining principle: "human infrastructure" recognizing that the strongest security frameworks depend on people who understand both technical risk and organizational culture. He translates complex cyber threats into actionable business intelligence for leadership teams, bridging the gap between security specialists and executive decision-makers.
