The People

About Us

A group is only as strong as the people in it. Each advisor brings decades of field experience, institutional trust, and a standard of delivery that does not accommodate shortcuts.

Founder

Julieta Reyes

Strategic Advisor | Diversity & Inclusion Lead | Global Operations

Julieta Reyes is a strategic advisor whose work sits at the juncture of diplomacy, defense, and organizational development. A cross-cultural leader shaped by field experience across North America, Europe, the Asia-Pacific, and the Caribbean, she brings a rare altitude-level perspective to the organizations she serves, one earned through her journey of navigating demanding institutional environments.

Her career took shape within the Kingdom of the Netherlands' diplomatic missions in the United States: first at the Consulate General in Miami, one of the most diverse and multicultural jurisdictions in the Americas, and later at the Embassy in Washington D.C., the capital of the Free World. Across both posts she built deep expertise in strategic communication, inclusive protocol design, and logistics modernization, serving as a trusted advisor to senior military leadership and heads of mission. She has led diversity and inclusion strategy at institutional level and carries one conviction into every engagement: a resilient organization is only as strong as the people it empowers.

Advisor

Ryan Barras

Director of IT Security / CISO | GRC & Strategic Operations

With extensive experience across the healthcare, government, and corporate sectors, Ryan Barras builds security programs that protect both data and business objectives. His approach goes beyond mere compliance; he specializes in maturing TPRM programs, automating SecOps via ServiceNow, and conducting high-level tabletop exercises with the CISA.

Whether leading a not-for-profit hospital system like Mt. Sinai or consulting for the US Department of Labor, Ryan prioritizes "human infrastructure" alongside technical resilience. A relationship developer at his core, he translates complex cyber risks into actionable business intelligence for executive leadership.
